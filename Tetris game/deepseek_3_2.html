HOW TO PLAY

Objective
Complete horizontal lines by rotating and moving the falling tetrominoes. Each completed line disappears and awards points.
Scoring
1 line = 100 × level
2 lines = 300 × level
3 lines = 500 × level
4 lines (Tetris) = 800 × level
Level Up
Every 10 cleared lines increases the level. Higher levels make pieces fall faster, increasing the challenge.
Hold Feature
Press C to hold the current piece for later use. You can only hold one piece at a time.
